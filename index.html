Get To Know Us

Founded in 2008, Brighter Beginnings sought out to complete one mission: Have a positive impact in the world. Children in particular are most vulnerable to the decisions that are made today. With pollution and anti-vaccine movements, limited educational resources it was clear that a positive impact should be instilled into those who will be able to. So, the journey began by taking one step at a time around the world...
